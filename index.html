Higan: Eruthyll Global CB Tier List

This tier list is created based upon Higan: Eruthyll Global Closed Beta package, and is for reference only. I am very open to any suggestions and changes - just give me a call at HE Official Discord @C86#8620 or submit a merge request here.

About Gopher Auxiliary of Eruthyll

We connect the land of Eruthyll with the earth. Founded as a fan-based organisation, we are keen on providing you with the best services possible that will allow directors to fully enjoy their journey with heroes at the Gopher Troupe. We provide services including free Eruthyll.net mailbox and the community-based Eruthyll Wiki. Gopher Auxiliary of Eruthyll is not a registered organisation, operation of which is guided by Bilibili HK Limited.

About Eruthyll.net email addresses

Anyone with a qualified existing mailbox can apply for one fully functional mailbox with 50GB of space that ends with @eruthyll.net.

Registration requires a verified email address from a list of major public email service providors including Microsoft, Apple and Google and does not require a human verification. Consequently, any email sent through any @eruthyll.net address, regardless how real they appear to be, are not from Gopher Auxiliary of Eruthyll or BILIBILI HK LIMITED.

Gopher Auxiliary of Eruthyll will only communicate using a @eruthyll.org address (it ends with .org instead of .net), while officials will only use @biligame.net or @eruthyll.com address to provide support or send out newsletters.

Your Eruthyll.net account is also needed to contribute to Eruthyll Wiki. Eruthyll.net account is not available for in-game registration or login.
